Skinshot Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2025

Skinshot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

How Skinshot Works

The extension only modifies the Steam inventory page to add a “Screenshot...” button for CS2 items. When clicked, the button copies the item’s Inspect link to the clipboard and opens Skinport’s screenshot generator.

Data Collection

Skinshot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It does not access or monitor your browsing history, communications, or sensitive information.

Third Parties

No user data is shared with third parties. The extension only opens screenshot.skinport.com with the Inspect link provided by Steam.
